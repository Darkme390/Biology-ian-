This the process by which cell divide inorder to multiply in number.

Forms of cell division
1.Mitosis
2.Meiosis

Mitosis
This is the form of cell devision were two deploid daughter cells are produced from on parend cell.

PHASES OF MITOSIS

Meiosis
This is the form of cell division where four haploid duagther cells are produced from one parent cell.

PHASES OF MEIOSIS

SIMILALITIES BETWEEN MEIOSIS AND MITOSIS
-Both form daugther cells
-Both need energy
-Both involve interphase,prophase,metaphase,anaphase and telophase
-In both a spindle is formed

DIFFERENCES BETWEEN MITOSIS AND MEIOSIS
-In mitosis two cells are formed while in meiosis four cells are produced
-In mitosis each cell formed has 46 chromosomes while in meiosis each cell formed has 23 chromosomes
